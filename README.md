# 🌞 Heliopath – Smart Solar Infrastructure

Heliopath is a concept-driven project focused on transforming unused infrastructure like roads and walkways into energy-generating systems using curved solar panel technology.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Heliopath introduces a **modular solar corridor system** designed to:
- Capture sunlight from multiple angles
- Generate renewable energy efficiently
- Utilize unused road space
- Support EV charging and smart infrastructure

---

## 🌍 Key Features

- ⚡ **Curved Solar Panels** for multi-angle sunlight capture  
- 🔋 **Energy Storage System** for continuous power supply  
- 🚗 **EV Charging Integration** along pathways  
- 🌦️ **Weather-Based Efficiency Prediction**  
- 📊 **Energy Dashboard (Prototype)**  
- 🧠 **Scalable System Design** for real-world deployment  

---

## 🏗️ System Flow

Sunlight → Curved Panels → Energy Conversion → Storage → Usage (EV Charging, Lighting, Infrastructure)

---

## 🗺️ Deployment Plan (CUK Campus)

This project includes a real-world implementation plan:

- 🏫 Boys Hostel → Psychology Department (750m)
- 🏫 Girls Hostel → Computer Science Department (700m)
- 📏 Road width: 7 meters
- ⚡ EV charging nodes every 100–150 meters
- 🔋 Battery units near academic zones

---

## 🧱 Development Phases

### Phase 1 – Structure

### Phase 2 – Energy System
Basic energy generation and storage logic

### Phase 3 – EV Integration
Charging stations along solar corridor

### Phase 4 – Smart System
Weather-based prediction and efficiency logic

### Phase 5 – Deployment
Real-world implementation and scalability

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

Urban infrastructure often wastes large surface areas that could be used for energy generation. At the same time, demand for renewable energy and EV charging is increasing.

---

## ✅ Solution

Heliopath provides a **cost-efficient, modular solar infrastructure system** that:
- Generates energy
- Provides shade
- Supports EV charging
- Scales across campuses, highways, and urban areas

---

## 💰 Cost Strategy

- Modular construction for easy installation  
- Use of standard materials  
- Reduced maintenance through passive design  
- Target: **30–35% cost reduction**

---
