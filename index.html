Why Heliopath?

Urban spaces waste massive potential for solar energy. Traditional solar systems require large land areas and fixed angles, reducing efficiency.

Heliopath solves this by integrating curved solar structures over roads and walkways, maximizing sunlight capture while utilizing existing infrastructure.

About Heliopath

Heliopath is a curved solar panel system designed to improve energy efficiency by capturing sunlight from multiple angles throughout the day.

It is planned for installation over roads or open spaces, making use of unused areas while generating clean energy. The structure also supports drainage, battery storage, and future smart integrations.
